## 👋 About Me

**I help businesses stop guessing and start deciding.**

I'm a Data Analyst specialising in Power BI, SQL, and Excel — with a background that spans operations, commercial environments, and customer-facing roles. That mix matters: I don't just build dashboards, I understand the *business problem* they're trying to solve before I touch a single dataset.

When a stakeholder says *"something's off with our retention"* — I know how to interrogate the data, isolate the signal from the noise, frame the finding clearly, and present it in a way that gets a decision made fast.

**Here's what that looks like in practice:**

- 📊 Built a **Power BI dashboard consolidating 6 data sources** — replacing manual weekly reporting with real-time KPI visibility for leadership
- 🔍 Analysed **5,000+ customer records** in Excel and identified the top 15% of customers driving **60% of total revenue** — insight that directly shaped retention strategy
- ⚡ Designed **Power Query workflows** that turned hours of manual data prep into a single refresh

I work well in fast-paced environments where data needs to be reliable, decisions need to be fast, and the margin for vagueness is low.

> 🎯 Open to **Data Analyst and BI Analyst roles** — remote, hybrid, or Sheffield-based. Available to contribute from day one.
